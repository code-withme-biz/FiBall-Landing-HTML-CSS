Welcome to Fi Ball!

Fi Ball is a unique and challenging soccer game available
on both iPhone and Android!

Shoot the ball and try and score yourself or use your teammates to score
around the moving goalie! Avoid the walls and referees and collect
gems for each level you successfully complete.

Having trouble passing a level?
No problem! You can use your hard earned gems to ‘Skip’ a level or
you can purchase additional gems to upgrade the ball style and progress
upwards within the Global leaderboard ranks!

Don’t forget to follow us on Instagram: @FiBallApp to learn more
about our weekly competitions and prizes!

Features:

  • Unique style gameplay  
  • Skills based control        
  • Multiple levels                 
  • Progressive gameplay    
  • Prizes and Competitions
